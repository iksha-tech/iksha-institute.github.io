Course Curriculum
  • Introduction to the Stock Market : Understanding market structure, participants, and how trading works.
  • Risk & Money Management : Essential principles to protect capital and maximize profits.
  • Candlestick Basics : Foundations of candlestick patterns and their significance.
  • Advanced Candlestick Secrets : Hidden insights for better market interpretation.
  • Reversal Patterns - I : Key candlestick signals for market reversals.
  • Reversal Patterns - II : Deep dive into advanced reversal formations.
  • Indicators & Their Applications : I : Understanding key technical indicators.
  • Indicators & Their Applications : II : Combining indicators for stronger analysis.
  • Trading Psychology & Mind-set Mastery : Overcoming emotional biases and building discipline.
  • Winning Strategies & Stock Selection : Proven techniques, screeners, and trade execution.
  • Price Action Mastery : Institutional trading secrets.
  • Chart Patterns & Their Significance : Identifying high-probability setups.
  • Fibonacci Retracement : Fibonacci levels for entry and exit points.
  • Divergence & Hidden Divergence : The secrets untold.
  • Introduction to Derivatives : Understanding futures and options.
  • Futures Trading Essentials : How futures contracts work and trading.
  • Options Trading Basics : Calls, puts, and how to leverage options for profits.
  • Reading the Option Chain : Analysing open interest, and implied volatility.
  • Trend Identification Using Derivative Data : Using futures and options data for market direction.
  • Mastering Derivative Trading : Trade derivatives with confidence.
  • Deep Dive into Risk & Money Management Psychology : Building a rule-based trading system. Mastering emotional control & decision-making.
  • Options Greeks Explained : Learn Delta, Theta, Vega, and Gamma.
  • Advanced Option Strategies : Spreads, Straddles, Strangles, and Iron Condors.
  • Hedging Portfolio with Derivatives : Managing risk in volatile markets.
  • Creating Zero-Loss (Low Risk) Strategies : Delta-neutral & gamma scalping techniques.
  • BTST/STBT & Options Trading.
  • Swing, Positional Trading - Combining candlesticks with key technical indicators.
